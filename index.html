About Dickson Antiquities

With over a decade of experience in curating exceptional antiques and uncommon objects, Dickson Antiquities specializes in sourcing unique pieces that tell a story. From Victorian-era collectibles to mid-century treasures, each item in our collection is carefully selected for its historical significance, craftsmanship, and aesthetic appeal.

We pride ourselves on authenticity, detailed documentation, and providing collectors with pieces they'll treasure for generations. Every item is photographed extensively and ships with care to ensure it arrives in perfect condition.
